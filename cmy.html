Cyan Magenta Yellow (CMY)
CMYK is an subtractive reflected light color model where each
color is stored in one byte.
All possible combinations of CMYK give 224 different colors.

Starting with white color, to which different color inks are added
to absorb (subtract) light that is reflected.
